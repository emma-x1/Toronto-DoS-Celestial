Welcome to Celestial, Toronto's Hackathon for Girls!

Introducing the first international Days of Service event! We hope to inspire girls to dream bigger and conquer the world of Computer Science... Join us in Toronto!

What is Celestial?

Celestial is a hackathon - you might have heard this term before! A hackathon is a social coding event - in other words, an event where participants work together on coding projects! Celestial is focused on teaching girls, young women, and non-binary youth - these groups are underrepresented in the tech industry, and we aim to make this field more accessible to them.

Celestial is a two-part event: the first part, in collaboration with Power to Girls, will take place in late August, 2024! The second part will take place in October (with a more spooky theme)!

Celestial is part of Days of Service, a program that has held similar educational hackathons across the United States! This is our first Canadian event, but we're hoping to host many more both in Canada and internationally.

What are we going to do at Celestial?

Our talented team is working hard on Celestial's workshops. You'll have the chance to choose one of the workshops, go through a step-by-step tutorial of the technology, then put your own spin on it! In August, our workshops are going to be printed circuit boards (PCBs) and 3D modelling. Both are exciting opportunities to learn about emerging hardware and software.

More about our workshops...

Our PCB (printed circuit board) workshop will teach you the basics of PCBs, the skeleton of most electronics! We'll go through how to design and test PCBs, then create a working PCB flashlight. You'll even have the opportunity to learn about how you can get one produced to take home!

Our 3D modelling workshop will teach you about creating your own 3D designs using Spline! You'll have a chance to explore the software and create your own digital designs - get creative!
